# Privacy Rules

## Principle

This is a private operating repository, but sensitive information should still be minimized.

## Do Not Store Unless Needed

- passport data;
- medical details;
- bank card numbers;
- passwords, tokens, seed phrases;
- private contact details copied without reason;
- unnecessary personal details about other people.

## Store Carefully

- financial obligations;
- family matters;
- health notes;
- legal documents;
- company-confidential details.

## Redaction Rule

If a task can be managed without raw sensitive data, store a summary and a pointer to the secure source instead of copying the sensitive data.
