# Intake Rules

## Goal

Сырой вход должен стать понятным артефактом, задачей или календарным событием.

## Intake Checklist

For each input, detect:

- people;
- dates and deadlines;
- money and assets;
- promises and next steps;
- decisions;
- risks;
- open questions;
- target domain: life, finance, project, work, company, people.

## Output

Each processed input should produce one of:

- routed note;
- task;
- meeting note;
- financial record;
- contact / follow-up;
- clarification request.

## Do Not

- Do not leave important outcomes only in chat.
- Do not invent facts that were not in the input.
- Do not route company material into personal life folders.
- Do not route personal/family sensitive material into company folders.
