# Life Plan Maintenance

## Targets

- Daily: `calendar/daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md`
- Weekly: `calendar/weekly/YYYY-Www.md`
- Monthly: `calendar/monthly/YYYY-MM.md`

## Workflow

1. Identify what changed.
2. Update stable facts if the change is durable.
3. Update tasks/waiting items if next actions changed.
4. Rebalance daily/weekly/monthly plan.
5. Move dropped items explicitly to later, waiting, or someday.
6. Keep a short change note in the plan.

## Rules

- Do not silently pile new work on top of old work.
- Protect medication reminders and hard appointments.
- Respect capacity and recovery.
- If priority is ambiguous, ask or create a clarification note.
