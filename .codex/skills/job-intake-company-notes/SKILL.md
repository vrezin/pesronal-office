# Job Intake Company Notes

## Artifact

`personal-projects/personal-brand/workspace/job-intake/COMPANY_NOTES.md`

## When To Use

- A new named company appears.
- A known company appears again and changes the view.
- Several unnamed roles form a reusable bucket, such as Web3 fintech, retail/POS, integrator, stability CTO, or AI-native product.
- A prior note needs correction after a new analysis.

## Note Contract

For each company or bucket, keep:

- what we think from intake;
- best lane / CV track;
- watchouts;
- related analysis links.

## Rules

- Notes are intake-derived hypotheses, not external due diligence.
- Do not browse unless explicitly asked or needed for current decision accuracy.
- Keep notes short; detailed evidence belongs in the linked analysis.
- Use cautious language for sensitive or uncertain companies.
