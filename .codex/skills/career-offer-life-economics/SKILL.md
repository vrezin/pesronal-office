# Career Offer Life Economics

## Inputs

Read:

- `finance/personal-budget/career-relocation-baseline.md`
- `life/health-lifestyle/lifestyle-facts/profile.md`
- vacancy analysis or JD being reviewed

Use current external market data when salary is absent or ambiguous.

## Output Block

Add a section to JD analysis:

`## Lifestyle / Relocation / Compensation`

Include:

- current location fit: Novosibirsk / remote / hybrid / onsite;
- relocation requirement and family impact;
- school/logistics impact for daughters;
- housing impact: apartment, house, mortgage, utilities, possible duplicate housing;
- adaptation costs: moving, rent/deposit, temporary travel, schooling, setup costs;
- market salary benchmark if vacancy salary is missing;
- target income: minimum acceptable, comfortable, and strong upside;
- verdict: improves, preserves, or worsens family quality of life.

## Rules

- Do not invent exact market salaries. If salary is missing, browse or clearly mark as unverified estimate.
- Use RUB/month gross unless the vacancy uses another format.
- Separate one-time relocation costs from recurring monthly costs.
- Treat Moscow/on-site relocation as a major life-cost risk by default.
- If the role is remote from Novosibirsk, explicitly note the lifestyle advantage.
- If schooling impact is unclear, list it as a question, not an assumption.
