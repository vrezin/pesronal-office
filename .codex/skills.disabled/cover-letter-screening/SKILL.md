# Cover Letter And Screening

- the relevant `job-intake/analyses/*-analysis.md` if the JD was already analyzed.

## Style

- Start with the employer's real pain.
- Use 4-6 evidence bullets when a full cover letter is needed.
- Keep short recruiter replies direct and practical.
- End with a useful question about team, scope, first 3-6 month impact, salary, format, or role balance.
- Answer in Russian unless the target text must be in English.

## Avoid

- `20+ years`, `23+ years`, `26+ years` in ordinary HH/ATS messages.
- Generic traits like "responsible" or "communicative".
- Overclaiming hands-on stack depth.
- Deep ML, GPU cluster, local inference, smart contract, wallet/MPC, or similar claims unless user supplied evidence.
- Sarcasm about strange JD wording.

## Risk Defusing

- Hands-on role: present playing manager / architecture-and-delivery mode.
- Small team: avoid opening with 200+ people or enterprise scale.
- AI role: emphasize AI implementation, LLM applications, RAG/agents, governance, and business process automation.
- Domain gap: bridge via similar process/scale patterns rather than pretending direct domain ownership.
