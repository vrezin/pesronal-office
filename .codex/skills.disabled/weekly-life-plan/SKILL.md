# Weekly Life Plan

## Inputs

Read only what is needed:

- `calendar/planning/personal-daily-weekly-planning.md`
- `life/health-lifestyle/health-facts/`
- `life/health-lifestyle/lifestyle-facts/`
- `calendar/weekly/` and `calendar/daily/`
- `tasks/active/` and `tasks/waiting/`
- relevant project/company/home files.

## Output

Create or update:

`calendar/weekly/YYYY-Www.md`

Include:

- week objective;
- fixed events and constraints;
- medication schedule summary from `health-facts/medications.md`;
- Saturday groceries and weekly menu from `meal-grocery-planning`;
- top personal/home blockers;
- business/company attention;
- daily focus outline;
- open questions.

## Planning Rules

- Plan from facts, not wishful thinking.
- Keep capacity realistic.
- Put uncertain health/lifestyle details into questions, not assumptions.
- Link tasks and source artifacts.
