## Vacancy / JD Intake

1. Save full JD to `job-intake/jd-archive/YYYY-MM-DD-company-role.md`.
2. Save analysis to `job-intake/analyses/YYYY-MM-DD-company-role-analysis.md`.
3. Use `job-intake/jd-analysis-template.md`.
4. Assign `Effort Class` (`A-class`, `B-class`, or `C-class`) before deciding whether to tailor anything.
5. Update `job-intake/INDEX.md`; make the class visible in `Priority` or `Next`.
6. Update `job-intake/COMPANY_NOTES.md` when company/bucket memory changes.

## Effort Class Gate

Use this gate to prevent vacancy processing from becoming an open-ended anxiety loop.

- `A-class`: rare role with strong fit, real upside, and a plausible path to interview. It is worth thinking, choosing words carefully, tailoring the cover letter, and possibly preparing a targeted resume package.
- `B-class`: normal plausible role. Use the best existing CV, send a short template cover letter, and cap pre-response work at roughly 10 minutes. Do not create a targeted resume before a human response unless the user explicitly asks.
- `C-class`: "maybe" role. Do not process deeply, do not tailor, and do not create active tasks unless the user has energy or asks to reopen it. Thin trace or park it.

Default downgrade rules:

- If legal/remote/relocation eligibility is unclear and likely blocking, downgrade by one class until clarified.
- If the role is only "interesting as a market signal", classify as `C-class` unless there is a concrete next action.
- If applying requires more than 10 minutes of customization and the role is not clearly `A-class`, do not customize.

## Analysis Must Include

- verdict;
- effort class;
- best CV;
- match reasons;
- risks;
- relocation/lifestyle/compensation;
- how to defuse risks;
- questions to ask;
- cover letter or screening answer when useful.

## CV Defaults

- AI / GenAI / automation / agents -> AI Transformation CV.
- Startup / first product / founding CTO -> Startup/Product Engineering CV.
- Large engineering org -> Heavy Enterprise CV.
- Digital modernization / transformation -> Digital Transformation CV.
- Stability / governance / SDLC -> Stability & Governance CV.
- Integrator / 1C / P&L / custom delivery -> Business Unit CV.

## HH Gmail Monitor

Use `automation/prompts/hh-gmail-monitor.md` for scheduled mail scans. Classify HH mail as status update, invitation, new vacancy, or noise. Invitations should create/update high-priority tasks and be starred/marked important when the Gmail connector allows it.

## Guardrails

- Do not browse HH by default.
- Do not invent salary, stack, domain, dates, or responsibilities.
- Always consider Novosibirsk family baseline, relocation cost, school logistics, and target income when role format or compensation matters.
