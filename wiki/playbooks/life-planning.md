# Life Planning Playbook

## Intake

Route life inputs into:

- health facts: `life/health-lifestyle/health-facts/`;
- lifestyle facts: `life/health-lifestyle/lifestyle-facts/`;
- tasks: `tasks/active/` or `tasks/waiting/`;
- calendar plans: `calendar/daily/`, `calendar/weekly/`, `calendar/monthly/`;
- processed trace: `inbox/processed/`.

## Weekly Planning

Build plans from existing facts, current obligations, active/waiting tasks, calendar constraints, and current company/personal projects.

Include only grounded items:

- medication reminders from saved instructions;
- Saturday groceries and weekly menu from preferences and constraints;
- daily/weekly/monthly plan corrections after new intakes.

## Safety

Do not invent medical treatment, medication dosage, diet restrictions, or training recommendations. If health input is incomplete, create a clarification note.
