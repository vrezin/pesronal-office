## Interpretation

This is a market-timing and positioning signal for the personal AI secretary line of thinking. It does not prove that `secretar_bot` should become a standalone product, but it does invalidate the strongest form of the old dismissal: "nobody needs this category."

Important boundary: user does not claim that Yandex stole the idea and recognizes that the idea was broadly "in the air." The painful part is that the user's earlier adjacent work had been packaged, rejected, and then the category was later validated by a major platform.

Potentially useful reframing:

- from "chatbot secretary" to "personal memory capture and action router";
- from generic assistant to trusted private operating layer;
- from one bot interface to cross-channel capture with searchable, editable memory;
- from consumer gadget competition to niche professional workflows where privacy, ownership, and customization matter.
