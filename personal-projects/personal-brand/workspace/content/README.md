# Personal Brand Content

This folder stores Vladimir's personal public-content drafts: LinkedIn posts, blog drafts, thought-leadership notes, and publication-ready copy.

## Rules

- Personal-author drafts live here, not in company workspaces.
- If a company board/review says a text must not be published as a corporate post, move the working version here and leave only a trace/link in the company workspace.
- Keep company/client/project-specific confidential context out of public drafts.
