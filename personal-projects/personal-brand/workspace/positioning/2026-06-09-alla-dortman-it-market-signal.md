# 2026-06-09 Alla Dortman IT Market Signal

Дата: 2026-06-09

Источник: user-provided longread by HR director Alla Dortman about the 2024-2025 IT hiring market.

## What The Signal Says

The market became denser and more selective:

- fewer public openings than in autumn 2024;
- more resumes per vacancy;
- stronger filtering by keywords, ATS and automated screening;
- more direct hiring, referrals and hidden hiring;
- more testing and case interviews;
- stronger demand for specific niches rather than generic "just a backend/frontend engineer".

The growing segments named in the article are:

- AI;
- cloud;
- information security;
- analytics;
- integration services.

## Implications For Personal Brand

The article supports a positioning move away from a broad, generic "senior tech leader" story toward a sharper market-facing package.

Better framing:

> AI-enabled enterprise CTO / transformation leader who can also do hands-on delivery.

Supporting sub-tracks:

- AI transformation / automation architect;
- enterprise integration / business unit leader;
- production stability / engineering governance;
- direct search / executive search for large-scale engineering leadership.

## What To Change In The CV Story

- Lead with one or two clear market categories, not a wide umbrella.
- Make the first screen look like a technical document, not a life story.
- Show concrete results, domain depth and current relevance.
- For HH / ATS, use compact, keyword-aligned wording.
- For direct search, keep the full scale narrative and enterprise credibility.
