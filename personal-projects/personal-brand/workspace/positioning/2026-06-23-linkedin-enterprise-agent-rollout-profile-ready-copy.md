## Positioning Decision

Yes: LinkedIn should be rebuilt around this sharper category.

The strongest market phrase is not generic `AI Transformation`. It is:

> enterprise agent rollout / AI deployment / forward-deployed AI implementation

LinkedIn can carry this better than HH because the top card and About section can explain the category before a recruiter sees the full work history.
