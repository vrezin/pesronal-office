# Experience Block Strategy

Цель: определить, как каждый блок опыта выглядит в разных версиях резюме: где раскрывать подробно, где оставить 1-2 строки, где свернуть в `Earlier career`, а где убрать.

## Legend

- `Full` - ключевой блок версии: 6-8 bullets, с метриками и контекстом.
- `Medium` - важный поддерживающий блок: 3-5 bullets.
- `Short` - 1-2 bullets внутри опыта.
- `One-line` - одна строка в `Earlier career` / `Additional background`.
- `Omit` - не использовать в этой версии.

## Resume Versions

1. `AI-First` - AI Transformation Lead / AI Automation Architect.
2. `Startup CTO` - Startup CTO / Co-founder CTO / Fractional CTO.
3. `Heavy Enterprise` - CTO / VP Engineering / Director of Engineering.
4. `Transformation` - Digital / Technology Transformation Director.
5. `Stability & Governance` - CTO / Head of Engineering для production stabilization / governance.
6. `Business Unit / Integration` - mini-CEO / mini-CTO для интеграторов, ERP/1C/enterprise automation.

## Global Rules

- Для HH / ATS не начинать с `26+ лет`, НГУ 1998, Запсибкомбанк 1999 или ЦФТ 2001.
- Для `Stability & Governance` не начинать с AI: AI только дополнительный asset.
- Для employee CV убрать блок “кого могу привести в проект”.
- Для executive/direct search можно раскрывать масштаб шире, но раннюю карьеру всё равно держать компактно.
- Для international CV клиентские имена типа Дом.рф / Школа семи гномов лучше обобщать.

## Fincomtech

### Use When

- AI-first;
- Startup CTO;
- Business Unit / Integration;
- Transformation, если нужно показать current AI / enterprise automation angle.

### AI-First: Full

Фокус: AI/automation platform, pilots, enterprise presale, delivery from idea to implementation.

```text
ООО «Финкомтех» — Technical Director / Co-founder
09.2023 — present

- Built the company’s initial product and engineering direction from scratch: product vision, first engineering team, initial delivery model.
- Designed the first platform architecture, cloud infrastructure and baseline DevOps processes.
- Worked directly with first customers, translating commercial commitments into realistic product roadmap and pilot scope.
- Led early enterprise presales: solution architecture, implementation model, delivery estimates and commercial framing.
- Brought the product from concept to pilot implementations with first customers, validating technical feasibility and commercial model.
```

### Startup CTO: Full

Фокус: 0 to 1, first team, first customers, PMF, founder mode.

```text
ООО «Финкомтех» — CTO / Co-founder
09.2023 — present

- Co-founded the company and shaped the initial product vision, engineering setup and operating model.
- Built the first engineering team and established delivery rhythm: prioritization, sprint cadence, release control.
- Worked with first customers to validate product-market fit and turn early commercial demand into a practical roadmap.
- Led architecture and delivery of the first platform version, including cloud infrastructure and DevOps basics.
- Supported enterprise presales and pilot planning, connecting customer needs, technical feasibility and commercial terms.
```

### Heavy Enterprise: Short

Фокус: current CTO / enterprise presale, без “маленького стартапа” на первом плане.

```text
ООО «Финкомтех» — CTO / Co-founder, 09.2023 — present
Built the initial product, engineering team and platform architecture; supported enterprise presales and pilot implementations for first customers.
```

### Transformation: Medium

Фокус: building operating model and automation platform from zero.

```text
- Built an initial product and engineering operating model from scratch: prioritization, delivery rhythm, release control.
- Designed the first platform architecture, cloud setup and DevOps baseline.
- Turned first customer needs into pilot roadmap and implementation model.
```

### Stability & Governance: Short

Фокус: current CTO experience, but not AI headline.

```text
Current CTO / co-founder experience: built initial product architecture, delivery process, release control and pilot implementation model for enterprise customers.
```

### Business Unit / Integration: Medium

Фокус: mini-CEO / mini-CTO, presale, commercial-to-delivery.

```text
- Built product and engineering direction from zero: team, roadmap, delivery model.
- Worked directly with first customers and enterprise presales.
- Translated commercial commitments into architecture, implementation model and pilot delivery plan.
```

