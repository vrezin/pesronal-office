# HH Experience Block Library

Status: working source for HH profile-level experience block variants.

Purpose: maintain reusable experience blocks that can be included/excluded in HH resume assemblies for specific vacancies.

## Guardrails

- Do not invent facts.
- Keep canonical dates, employers, role names and metrics aligned with `master-profile.md`.
- Different blocks may emphasize different truths, but must not contradict each other.
- Use exact employer language when it is an honest bridge.

## Do-Not-Claim List

- Do not claim direct hands-on ML model training ownership unless the JD only needs production AI workflow / adoption leadership.
- Do not claim deep MLOps platform ownership beyond production-readiness, monitoring needs, quality gates, traceability and human escalation.
- Do not claim direct industry experience in MFO/agro/jewelry unless framed as adjacent fintech/logistics/retail/process transformation.
