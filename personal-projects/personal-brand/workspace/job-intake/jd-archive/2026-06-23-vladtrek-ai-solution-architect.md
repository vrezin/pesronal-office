## JD Summary

ВЛАДТРЕК развивает направление автоматизации и внедрения AI в бизнес-процессы. Цель роли - не просто писать код, а видеть процессы бизнеса целиком, предлагать решения и участвовать в построении AI-инфраструктуры компании.

Main responsibilities:

- analyze current business processes and identify automation opportunities;
- design, configure and implement AI agents for different business tasks;
- build internal and external AI assistants;
- integrate AI solutions with CRM, websites, messengers, databases and internal systems;
- design interaction scenarios between AI agents;
- configure workflow and automation logic;
- analyze implementation effectiveness and optimize processes.

Possible agent directions:

- AI copywriter;
- AI designer;
- AI performance agent;
- AI lead qualifier;
- AI analyst;
- sales assistant;
- document-flow assistant;
- customer-service assistant;
- internal-department assistants.

Requirements and useful signals:

- practical AI tools and automation experience;
- understanding of LLMs and AI agents;
- API experience;
- process automation and service-interaction logic;
- ability to analyze business tasks and propose solutions;
- system thinking.
