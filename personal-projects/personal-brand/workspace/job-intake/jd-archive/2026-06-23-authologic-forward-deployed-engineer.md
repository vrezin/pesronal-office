## Company

Authologic builds identity infrastructure for the digital economy: a full-stack e-ID Hub / orchestration platform that enables enterprises to verify users globally through a single API.

The platform connects identity providers, banking systems, eID schemes and verification services for enterprise customers in financial services, fintech, gaming, telecom and regulated industries.

## Role

Forward Deployed Engineer bridging enterprise customers and engineering.

Hybrid role combining:

- backend engineering;
- enterprise deployment;
- solution architecture;
- presales technical credibility;
- customer implementation ownership.

## Responsibilities

- Lead enterprise implementations and onboarding from architecture conversation to production go-live.
- Own technical delivery for strategic enterprise customers.
- Design integration strategies for complex enterprise and legacy environments.
- Map Authologic APIs into internal business operations and user journeys.
- Build rapid, production-grade prototypes and POCs.
- Run technical workshops and live integration sessions.
- Participate in executive-level customer meetings.
- Design identity and payment architectures.
- Integrate into MFA/SCA, banking, payments, fraud/risk/transaction monitoring and onboarding workflows.
- Advise on EUDI Wallet / eIDAS 2.0 compliant flows.
- Bring customer feedback back into product and help decide where custom delivery ends and core product starts.

## Requirements

- 8+ years software engineering, solution architecture or hands-on implementation.
- Strong backend background: Java, Kotlin, Node.js or similar.
- APIs, webhooks, event-driven architecture, distributed systems.
- Cloud platforms, Docker, Kubernetes.
- Ability to write integration code live during workshops / debugging sessions.
- Regulated entities: banks, fintechs, insurance or payment providers.
- Identity verification, onboarding, payment authorization, fraud mitigation.
