- lead backend, frontend, mobile, DevOps, Data Engineering and AI/ML teams;
- build engineering culture around results, responsibility and quality;
- hire, grow and mentor tech leads and key engineers;
- shape team structure and responsibility zones;
- develop SDLC from idea to stable production;
- improve delivery speed without losing quality and control;
- develop CI/CD, engineering practices and quality gates;
- control architecture decisions, code quality and service stability;
- implement AI tools for development acceleration and quality improvement;
- optimize engineering processes with AI assistants;
- participate in ML/AI product functionality;
- work with CPO on roadmap, feature risk and technical trade-offs;
- present technical vision and key decisions to leadership;
- control AWS infrastructure and microservice ecosystem;
- scale services for MAU and data-volume growth;
- ensure SLO, monitoring, resilience and security.
