## Company

Datafold builds a data engineering automation platform focused on data quality, data diffing, lineage, metadata, CI/CD workflow integration, and proactive shift-left testing for data engineering.

Customers include Patreon, Thumbtack, Substack and AngelList. Datafold raised USD 22M from YC, NEA and Amplify Partners.

## Role

Forward Deployed Data Engineer owning AI-automated data platform migration projects.

Datafold delivers large-scale data engineering projects, especially data platform migrations, at fixed price and guaranteed timeline using proprietary AI tooling. They partner with Databricks and Snowflake.

## Responsibilities

- Own 1-4 concurrent migration projects end-to-end: scoping, planning, execution, customer handoff.
- Be the primary customer contact: weekly check-ins, stakeholder management, risk escalation.
- Configure Datafold's Migration Agent and oversee migration execution.
- Partner with engineering team to execute migrations.
- Help refine and scale product and delivery playbook.

## Requirements

- 3-6 years in data consulting, professional services, or customer-facing data engineering.
- Strong communication across executive check-ins and technical design sessions.
- Strong grasp of modern data stack: dbt, Snowflake, Databricks, orchestration tools, stored procedures, streaming, incremental processing.
- Extreme ownership.
- AI power user.
- Exposure to legacy data stack and data platform migration is a strong plus.

## Technology

Python, FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, Celery, Rust, PostgreSQL, Neo4j, ClickHouse, TypeScript, React, Redux, GraphQL, WebAssembly.
