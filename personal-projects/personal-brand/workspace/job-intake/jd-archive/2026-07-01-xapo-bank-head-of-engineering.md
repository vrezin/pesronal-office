## JD Summary

Xapo Bank is creating a function-level Head of Engineering role reporting to the CTO. The role owns engineering standards, unified SDLC governance, backend change-management controls, delivery predictability, people leadership of Engineering Managers, engineering hiring, incident response, post-mortems, tech-debt roadmap, capacity planning, and AI-assisted engineering adoption.

The engineering organization is organized around three product tribes: Growth, Payments, and Assets Under Management. Each tribe is run day-to-day by an Engineering Manager. The Head of Engineering is accountable for the function-level engineering bar and partners with Architecture, Quality Engineering, Security, Frontend Engineering, and Platform Engineering.

Xapo frames the environment as a Bitcoin-native regulated bank where defects can affect customer Bitcoin and customer dollars. The role therefore emphasizes audit-readiness, regulated change control, observability, incident discipline, and security/quality standards.

## What The Role Owns

- Unified Software Development Lifecycle definition, governance, evolution, and enforcement.
- Engineering standards across code review, retrospectives, internal quality discipline, and feedback loops.
- People leadership of Engineering Managers, including performance, growth, 1:1 cadence, and hiring.
- Controlled and audited backend change-management process for material changes.
- Function-level tech-debt roadmap and engineering capacity planning.
- Engineering incident response, structured post-mortems, and closure of action items.
- AI-first engineering standard: coding assistants, AI-assisted code review, AI-assisted test generation, real adoption, and measurable productivity benefits.
- Delivery predictability across the engineering function, with an explicit target of shipping committed scope on committed dates at a 90% bar.

## Skills / Requirements

- 8+ years in software engineering.
- 3-4+ years in engineering leadership across multiple teams or squads.
- Experience managing Engineering Managers, not only individual senior engineers.
- Function-level delivery predictability ownership.
- Hands-on engineering-practice background: SDLC, code-review standards, change management, retrospectives, feedback loops.
- People leadership: hiring, developing, and performance-managing EMs.
- Evidence-based point of view on AI-assisted development across the SDLC.
- Backend distributed-systems understanding in a regulated, high-stakes environment.
- Engineering metrics ownership: delivery predictability, cycle time, defect escape rate, blocker aging.
- Strong written and verbal communication with technical and non-technical audiences.
- Regulated environment experience such as fintech, banking, payments, or equivalent.
