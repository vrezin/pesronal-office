## Company

Parsewise builds multi-document processing for insurance and reinsurance risk teams. Underwriters, brokers and risk teams use Parsewise to read full submission and claims files end-to-end, turn them into structured, source-linked, decision-ready data, and surface risk signals in a dedicated workflow UI.

They emphasize reliable/scalable unstructured-data pipelines, hallucination control, explainability and scalability beyond basic chatbot/document ingestion.

## Role

First Forward Deployed Engineer.

Own customer journey from first deployment to durable value:

- onboard insurance and reinsurance customers from first data ingestion to first decision-ready output;
- deploy and configure platform in customer environments;
- integrate with customer stacks and workflows;
- translate underwriting, claims and broking documents into working Parsewise configurations;
- join commercial conversations, scope use cases, run demos and pilots;
- build deployments that prove out pilots;
- bring customer lessons back into product.
