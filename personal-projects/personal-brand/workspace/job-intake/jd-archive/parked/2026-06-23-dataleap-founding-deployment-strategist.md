## Role

Lead Dataleap rollout across the European market from Munich, working directly with the CEO/founder.

The role is framed as trusted partner / AI transformation operator, not account manager or vendor.

- Diagnose where AI creates leverage inside customer organizations.
- Identify high-value use cases.
- Train the organization.
- Build agents alongside customers and scale adoption org-wide.
- Host agent hackathons.
- Discuss rollout strategy with C-suite.
- Navigate enterprise politics, champions, blockers and relationships from end users to CEO.
- Bring customer feedback to engineering/product.
- Build agents/workflows in Dataleap to automate the role itself.
- Lead European rollout from Munich.
- Build the team underneath the role as Dataleap scales.
