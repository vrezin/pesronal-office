## Posting Body

Head of Engineering (route to CTO) | UK, Remote First | GBP 120K equity

A hands-on Head of Engineering seat with a mapped route to the CTO title in 12-18 months. The role targets someone who has lived the Seed to Series A journey and wants the top technical seat with real traction underneath it.

The company is a UK AI startup led by Imperial and Cambridge-trained founders. It was bootstrapped to GBP 1M ARR and profitability with real customers before raising GBP 2.7M in seed funding.

The technical problem is fusing fragmented mobility data from cameras, sensors, and roadside infrastructure into an operational intelligence layer for UK transport networks. The work spans data platform engineering, geospatial modelling at scale, and AI recommendations under real operational constraints. The product is deployed with national infrastructure operators, transport authorities, and engineering consultancies that run the roads.

The next 18-24 months are about scaling to GBP 5M ARR and raising Series A. The Head of Engineering is expected to make that possible from the engineering side.

## Role Shape

- Hands-on scope: write code, run spikes, own architecture, and own the release lifecycle end to end.
- Team management is lightweight: 1:1s, growth conversations, and hiring input.
- This is not a role for someone who wants the CTO seat in order to stop shipping code.
- The inherited team is 7 strong ICs with Oxford, Imperial, Manchester, and Bath credentials plus prior Wayflyer and ASOS experience.
- The team is already writing majority AI-generated code and building agentic systems that support their own development.
- The mandate is to industrialise that advantage.
- CTO title is earned in 12-18 months through demonstrated CEO-trusted ownership and delivery against Series A / ARR targets.

## Candidate Profile

- Led engineering through a Seed to Series A/B scaling arc at a B2B SaaS product company.
- Managed at least 5 engineers with real hiring and delivery ownership.
- Built and owned systems where data volume, quality, and modelling are the product.
- Shipped customer-facing AI to production in the last 18 months and can discuss evaluation approach.
- Uses AI coding tools daily and has driven adoption across a team.
- Can handle enterprise customer calls with serious technical questions.
- Comfortable across React, TypeScript, Python, FastAPI, Dagster, dbt, Snowflake, AWS, or able to get there fast.

## Compensation And Logistics

- GBP 100,000-120,000 base.
- 0.5-0.75% equity with clear vesting.
- UK-based remote.
- 2-4 in-person team days per month in London or Bristol.
- Private health and pension.
- Application route: LinkedIn Easy Apply or email `reuben@techmunity.io`.
