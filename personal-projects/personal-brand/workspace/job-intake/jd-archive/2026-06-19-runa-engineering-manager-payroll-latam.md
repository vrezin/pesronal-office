## Role

Runa describes the role as a product engineer / Engineering Manager hybrid:

- owns development and rollout for an entire product or large project;
- champions process such as Scrum and TDD;
- writes technical specs and identifies risks before major projects;
- sets standards and reduces complexity;
- may take on tech lead / EM responsibilities;
- may lead teams of 2-5 engineers through a project;
- accountable for technical outcome and work quality.

Engineering Managers at Runa:

- lead engineers and support growth/performance management;
- partner with delivery managers, tech leaders and product owners;
- lead product delivery for internal and external customers;
- build teams, tools and processes for excellent execution;
- focus on developer experience, tools and pipelines.
