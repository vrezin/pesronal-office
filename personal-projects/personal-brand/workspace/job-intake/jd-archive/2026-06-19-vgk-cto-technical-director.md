## Position

The company is looking for a CTO who can take the technical function to the next level.

The role owns:

- technical vision;
- architecture;
- technology stack decisions;
- engineering practices;
- development organization;
- DevOps;
- data;
- team leads;
- technical evolution of modern B2B products.

The JD explicitly says AI and agent technologies are an integral part of current and future products.

## Team

The CTO will manage a team of around 10 people, including team leads and specialists across development directions.

## Responsibilities

- Form and implement company technology strategy.
- Build modern product and platform architecture.
- Make key architecture decisions balancing speed and long-term sustainability.
- Develop engineering culture and development processes.
- Manage engineering teams.
- Increase development speed and reduce time to market.
- Ensure stability, reliability and scalability of products.
- Integrate AI, LLM and agent technologies into product strategy.
- Work with client technical teams and justify architecture decisions.
- Participate in B2B product development and technical evolution.

## Requirements

- Experience as CTO, Head of Engineering, Chief Architect or technical leader of a large development area.
- Engineering-team management from 5-7 people and more.
- Experience creating and developing B2B products.
- Architecture decisions with production responsibility.
- Understanding of modern software-development approaches and engineering-organization design.
- Corporate technology implementation experience.
- Strong orientation in modern technologies, architecture approaches and open-source ecosystem.
- Understanding of capabilities and limitations of modern AI solutions, LLMs and agent systems.
- Ability to balance development speed and architecture quality.
- Ability to build processes for long-term product development and support.
- Strong communication with developers and clients.
