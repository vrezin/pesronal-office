## Company

Noora Health trains patient families with health skills through health-system partners. The organization says it has trained more than 58 million caregivers and patients across 16,300+ facilities in India, Bangladesh, Indonesia, and Nepal.

The YC company profile lists Noora Health as a 500-person healthcare organization based in Bengaluru, India. The founder listed on YC is Edith Elliott, whose background is described around research, policy, design and global health systems; the profile also references Ashoka, Mulago, DRK, Echoing Green, Stanford, Ariadne Labs at Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health, TED 2022, Audacious Project and Skoll Foundation recognition.

Its Care Companion Program is described as reducing:

- post-surgical cardiac complications by 71%;
- maternal complications by 12%;
- newborn complications by 16%;
- newborn mortality by 18%.

Noora Health says it is an Audacious Project Grantee and received the Skoll Foundation Award for Social Innovation.

## Role Context

Noora Health is an 11+ year organization with deep government relationships and operations across four countries. Technology is expected to play a larger role in supporting families, health workers, and public-health institutions.

The technology function is described as nascent and rapidly evolving:

- engineering team currently around 14 engineers;
- live but evolving conversational AI platform;
- hard unsolved problems around safe and reliable AI in high-stakes health contexts;
- users may have variable literacy and intermittent connectivity.

## Key Responsibilities

### Technology Strategy And Architecture

- Define and own architecture for a platform intended to reach 100 million caregivers.
- Build for resilience in low-connectivity environments.
- Support interoperability with government health systems across country contexts.
- Partner with product leadership to translate product strategy into scalable, secure, high-quality technical systems.
- Communicate technical vision to team, funders, partners, and other stakeholders.

### Engineering Leadership

- Lead and grow a high-performing engineering organization.
- Scale the current ~14-engineer team thoughtfully as the platform grows.
- Foster quality, speed, technical rigor, and a mission-driven engineering culture.

### Responsible AI

- Own responsible AI as a technical and ethical discipline.
- Build systems where model behavior is predictable, auditable, and safe in high-stakes health contexts.
- Understand failure modes before deployment.
- Protect communities served by the technology.

### Security And Governance

- Own technical governance across digital health products and technology systems.
- Partner with legal, product, and country teams on relevant standards and regulatory frameworks across countries.

### Government And External Partnerships

- Represent Noora Health in technical conversations with government stakeholders, funders, technology partners, and global health collaborators.
