You will play a key role in helping grow the company to its next level and shape its future — through hands-on technical execution. As a fractional CTO, you’ll lead a team of 2x passionate engineers, make meaningful product changes to grow it, solve complex challenges successfully, and deliver products that make a real impact.

This is your opportunity to combine technical expertise with inspirational leadership & shaping our product & technical landscape.


About us:

RapidSeedbox is a remote SaaS company with a mission to provide online anonymity to people worldwide.

We've been operating since 2008, serving thousands of active clients. With a clear vision for the future, our remote, passionate, and diverse team is continuously expanding, and we are on the lookout for talented individuals to join us on our exciting journey.



Our core values are:

- Excellence in everything we do (“I go above and beyond”)

- Ownership and responsibility (“I own my area of responsibility”)

- Continuously push the limits (“I don’t accept no as an answer. No is just an option”)

- Openness & cooperation (“Everyone matters”)


About the Role (Mission of the role):


As a fractional CTO you will be leading our engineering team as we decouple our legacy customer area (WHMCS / PHP) into a modern web app.

This is a hands-on technical leadership role: You’ll write code most days, guide architecture and execution, and be the single engineering counterpart to the PM/CEO to translate product requirements into a deliverable plan.


Hands-on vs leadership

Expect roughly 50% hands-on engineering (coding, architecture, code reviews) and 50% leadership (planning, mentorship, delivery accountability).


Reporting

You’ll work closely with the CEO/PM (and collaborate with the rest of the engineering team).


Your First Mission: Diagnosis (First 30 Days)

You’ll perform a deep technical + product diagnosis of our end-to-end — Tech-stack evaluation, WHMCS dependencies, billing flows, and internal tooling & customer needs.


You must be able to:

- Explain root causes vs surface symptoms clearly.

- Recommend exactly how to tackle our tech-stack limitations in the most agile & lean way possible.

- Create a 90-day execution plan that aligns technical realities with business goals


Key Responsibilities


Technical leadership (You set the standard)

- Own technical direction for the client-area/tech-stack decoupling

- Establish engineering best practices: code quality, reviews, testing strategy, definition of done

- Implement AI in the tech team throughout the full development cycle to speed up development time


Delivery & Planning (You make execution predictable)

- Translate product goals into an execution plan: milestones, sequencing, risks, and dependencies

- Break down initiatives into tasks the team can execute independently

- Track progress with lightweight and real KPIs.


Team Leadership (You level up the team)

- Mentor developers through feedback and coaching

- Run effective 1:1s and create clarity on expectations and growth

- Serve as a technical leader, combining hands-on development with strategic oversight.


Hands-on engineering (You ship)

- Implement critical parts of the new client area + middleware layer

- Review PRs, improve legacy code safely, and guide refactors

- Help the team move faster through better tooling and patterns (including AI-assisted workflows while maintaining quality)


Helping Product Outcomes

- Be involved in defining the product roadmap & team expectations

- Be involved with ICP interviews, customer discovery, translate insights into product & tech priorities.


You’ll be a great fit if you have the following hard skills:

- Deep Technical Skills: You possess very deep technical skills which helps you bridge the gap between developers & product.

- AI + Coding: Ability to leverage AI tools for fast iteration while maintaining high quality.

- System Thinking: You understand how complex technical systems fit together.

- Business Understanding: You understand the business side of things and You translate tech language to CEO language well.

- Excellent Communication: You communicate clearly, ask smart questions early, and surface risks before they become outages

- (Bonus) Product Excellence: You can relate and understand customer needs, uncover root problems, and help provide a voice to validate solutions before building.


You’ll be a great fit if you have the following personality traits:

- Start-up Mentality: You love wearing multiple hats, happy to own both the technical decisions and the product direction, while working in small teams.

- Hands-on approach: You are ready to roll up your sleeves & delve deep into intricate details of the technology & product.

- Customer First Mentality: You understand and care about customer pains, use cases, and workflows. You understand that products & features are built from the customer's point of view rather than your own.

- Results Oriented: You take full responsibility for outcomes — not tasks. You naturally step into gaps, solve problems proactively, and operate like a founder inside the product.

- High Technical Passion: You’re naturally passionate about technology. You love learning how systems work — and actively seek technical depth.

- Product-minded: You are commercially aware & product aware. You understand that engineering need to deliver business outcomes at the end of the day.


You’ll be a great fit if you have the following career background:

- 7+ years of hands-on software engineering experience, preferably in fast-paced startup environments.

- 3+ yrs of team leadership experience of an engineering team, as a CTO/VP/Head of engineering type roles.

- 2+ years of experience in a broader business or product involvement — not just backlog & technical delivery.

- Fluent in English, both written and verbal.

Your voice will be heard. You’ll work closely with leadership and have the space to experiment, learn, and make a visible impact.
