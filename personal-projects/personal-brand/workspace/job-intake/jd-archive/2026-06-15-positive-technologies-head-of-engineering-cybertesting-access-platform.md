## JD

ТризТех is a new vendor inside Positive Technologies Group. From October 2025 it is responsible for developing PT NGFW, a flagship network product.

The team is building a platform for authorized cybertesting through the internet in a controlled, safe and auditable way. The core engineering task is a distributed network-access management system where security, observability and predictability are architectural properties.

Current project stage:

- product concept is defined;
- detailed architecture concept exists;
- platform responsibility boundaries are defined;
- delivery stages are decomposed into PoC, MVP, piloting and production launch;
- strong product leader is responsible for product and piloting.

The new technical leader may confirm, simplify or revise architectural choices after PoC and pilot requirements.

Responsibilities:

- own technical strategy, architecture, engineering team and first platform releases;
- define minimum sufficient architecture for PoC and MVP;
- test key hypotheses around electronic signature, dataplane, policy application and network accounting;
- form technical roadmap and stage readiness criteria;
- build the core engineering team;
- justify vacancies internally and to candidates;
- organize development and engineering processes;
- personally dive into critical implementation areas, review and set quality requirements;
- bring VPN to a controlled pilot with real researchers and testing targets;
- prepare the platform for scaling and operations.

The role is not measured by the amount of personally written code, but early hands-on participation is expected in architecture, prototypes, critical components and technical decisions.

Engineering areas:

- control plane for distributed nodes;
- node-management agent and protected control channel;
- dynamic ACL, NAT, routing and network-limit policies;
- lifecycle of access profiles, sessions and public IP addresses;
- flow/accounting data collection and correlation;
- safe behavior during orchestrator, log collector or node-connection failures;
- researcher identification and action confirmation through УКЭП / GOST;
- RBAC, audit of sensitive operations and protection of legally significant actions;
- monitoring, SLI/SLO, load and degradation tests.

Requirements:

- experience launching a new technical product or SaaS/PaaS platform from idea to pilot;
- engineering team management from 5 people: hiring, development, motivation and product releases;
- strong backend, platform or system engineering background, 6+ years directly programming rather than only managing;
- distributed-system design and architecture decisions with operations in mind;
- Linux;
- modern networking: routing, NAT, ACL, VPN and stateful behavior;
- security mindset: trust boundaries, least privilege, compromise scenarios, safe failures;
- ability to reduce uncertainty through prototypes and experiments;
- ability to explain technical decisions to product and stakeholders.

Nice to have:

- understanding of pentesters, red team and bug bounty researchers;
- audit systems and large network-event processing;
- PKI, electronic signature and Russian УКЭП specifics;
- distributed systems operations and incident readiness;
- practical use of AI tools to accelerate development and testing.

Conditions:
