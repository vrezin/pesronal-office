## JD

The company is looking for a person who can help rebuild the organization into an AI-native company.

The goal is not to create another AI department or write a long strategy document. The company wants to implement AI into real business processes now, with fast implementations and measurable business effect in weeks and months, not years.

Responsibilities:

- find processes that can be automated with AI;
- launch internal AI products and AI agents;
- form AI strategy through practical implementations;
- build and develop an AI development team;
- reduce time from idea to launched solution;
- work directly with the CEO and function leaders;
- build an AI-first culture inside the company.

Expected background:

- experience launching AI products in production;
- team management experience;
- experience implementing AI into real business processes;
- high decision speed and results orientation;
- entrepreneurial mindset;
- willingness to do a lot hands-on at the start.

Nice to have:

- AI startup experience;
- international market experience;
- experience creating AI agents, GenAI products, and AI automation platforms.

Main success criterion:

- not the number of presentations or pilots, but the number of processes and products that started working differently because of AI.
