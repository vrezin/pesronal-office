Anyfin

We're not looking for a traditional CTO

Stockholm, Sweden. Reposted 5 days ago. 100+ people clicked apply.

Office-based. Full-time.

We are looking for someone who leads and sets technical direction in equal measure. Someone who can make hard calls, navigate business trade-offs, and still have useful conversations about architecture, credit decisioning, or how AI is changing the way great engineering teams work.

The foundation is built. The real expansion starts now.

Anyfin has helped many people in debt take back control of their finances. The company is moving to a larger scale and extending the journey toward lasting financial stability and wellbeing across Europe. It is profitable and regulated as a kreditmarknadsbolag, a licence that lets it operate across the EU. Germany is growing fast and Sweden and Finland are strong markets. The 2030 ambition is to be a pan-European company.

The team is small and intentionally so. The company is not trying to build a massive organization, but wants fewer people who want to do exceptional things, trust each other, move fast and care about doing things properly.

AI is a big part of how the company works. Not just as strategy, but lived across engineering, analytics, operations and product development. The company is building internal tooling on frontier models and believes the way strong product and engineering organizations operate is changing quickly.

What the role actually is

- Own the full technology function.
- Set direction, build the team, make trade-offs between speed and quality.
- Be a real contributor in leadership discussions about the business, not just technical matters.
- Treat the CTO scope as how technology creates value across every part of the business.
- Understand how engineering decisions shape customer behavior, product experience, expansion, compliance and competition.
- Stay close to the work, not necessarily writing production code every day, but maintaining genuine technical proximity.
- Hold engineering, business, AI, regulatory and organizational problems together: credit decisioning across markets, payments infrastructure, international scaling, multilingual AI systems, customer behavior, and team building.

What matters

- Prior leadership-team experience and decisions affecting the whole business.
- Managed other leaders and scaled an organization from scrappy to structured.
- Experience scaling a modern B2C product company in fintech, lending, payments or another regulated financial environment.
- Strong business understanding and ability to have real opinions beyond technical topics.
- Strong leadership without politics.
- Systems thinking, trade-off judgment and comfort with incomplete information.
- Care about impact, not only shipping.
- Genuine excitement about AI, actively building with it and thinking about what it changes for products, engineering teams and financial services.

Tech stack

TypeScript, Python, Go, GraphQL, React Native, Kubernetes, BigQuery and GCP. The stack matters less than the judgment brought to it.

Location

The role is based in Stockholm. The company says leadership needs to be embedded, close to teams and part of how culture develops. This is not negotiable.

Offer

Competitive salary and meaningful equity. The company is profitable and growing.

Anyfin is a fintech on a mission to simplify loan repayments and improve the financial wellbeing of millions. It helps consumers reduce the costs of high-interest loans and take control of repayment plans.
