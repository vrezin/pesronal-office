## LinkedIn Mismatch Interpretation

The gap between Redvio Care's visible healthcare staffing / property footprint and a `$5M` Web3/GameFi/staking technical-advisor search is too large to treat as normal recruiting context.

Plausible explanations, from least to most concerning:

1. Lica is informally helping someone outside her public business network and did not explain the mandate.
2. Redvio Care / Lica is being used as a warm-looking outreach channel for a third-party project.
3. The LinkedIn account is compromised or partially controlled by someone else.
4. The outreach is a credibility-harvesting or scam flow: get a senior technical profile into a call, obtain CV/personal details, request endorsement/advisor listing, or associate the user's name with a risky token/staking project.

This does not prove account compromise or fraud, but it raises the screening bar. The first reply should avoid sending CV, phone, documents, wallet details, or calendar access. It should ask for legal entity, official project email/domain, Redvio mandate, founder/team identity, compensation in fiat vs tokens/equity, audit reports, contract addresses, and whether public advisor listing/promotion is expected.
