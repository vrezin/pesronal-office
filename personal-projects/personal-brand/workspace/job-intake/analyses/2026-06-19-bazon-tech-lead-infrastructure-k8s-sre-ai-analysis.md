## Risks

- It asks for 5+ years in DevOps/SRE/Platform and production Kubernetes, IaC, CI/CD, Prometheus/Grafana/Loki/ELK/Tempo/Jaeger, Linux/networking and Go/Python/Bash automation.
- This may screen for a current senior SRE/platform engineer, not a CTO/engineering director who can govern the function.
- Office in Novosibirsk is good geographically but still likely office-first.

## Lifestyle / Relocation / Compensation

- Current location fit: strong if office in Novosibirsk is acceptable.

## Risk Mitigation

- Lead with reliability, delivery governance, incident process, engineering standards and AI-assisted operations.
