## Risks

- Risk 1: Overqualification and age/long-career signal. The JD asks for 1-3 years and may screen for a much cheaper hands-on prompt engineer.
- Risk 2: Replit-only execution. If the employer expects all work strictly inside Replit and treats this as a platform operator role, broader CTO/AI transformation experience may not matter.
- Risk 3: Moscow on-site/hybrid and salary hidden. Practical fit depends on compensation and required office cadence.

## Risk Mitigation

- Mitigation 1: Frame as "hands-on AI builder / technology leader" and say directly that you are comfortable taking raw hypotheses to MVP when there is clear business value.
- Mitigation 2: Avoid leading with full career length or 200+ engineer scope; keep the first response focused on AI prototypes, product hypotheses and practical delivery.
- Mitigation 3: Ask early about salary range, remote/hybrid cadence, seniority expectations, and whether Replit is a hard constraint or a preferred rapid-prototyping environment.
