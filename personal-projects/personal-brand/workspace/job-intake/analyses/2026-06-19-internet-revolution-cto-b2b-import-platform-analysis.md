## Short Verdict

Strong role shape, but not the current primary AI lane.

This is a serious 0-to-1 building CTO vacancy for a B2B import / marketplace-operations platform inside a revenue-generating e-commerce company. It is materially better than random startup chaos: the company claims 1.5+ billion RUB revenue, existing operational expertise, internal infrastructure already tested for 5 years, remote work, and a concrete business model.

The fit is strongest through product engineering, integration architecture, delivery setup, team formation, and TCO discipline.

The mismatch is equally clear: the role is not AI Product Engineer / AI Solution Architect. It pulls back into classic CTO territory, hands-on product build, hiring, process, budget, and management. That may be useful for stable income, but it does not advance the new external signal as cleanly as AI transformation / AI solution roles.

## Questions Before / During First Contact

1. What salary range and bonus/equity/profit-share are planned for the CTO role?
2. Is remote work fully remote from Novosibirsk, or are Moscow visits expected?
3. What stack assumptions already exist, if any?
4. How hands-on is "hands-on" during the first 3-6 months: architecture/code review/key modules, or regular feature delivery?
5. Who owns product management: CTO, CPO, CEO, or a separate product lead?
6. What budget is approved for the first 3-6 hires?
7. Which existing internal systems must be reused versus rebuilt?
8. Is there appetite to use AI automation / AI-native SDLC to accelerate requirements, testing, support, knowledge base, logistics docs and integrations?
