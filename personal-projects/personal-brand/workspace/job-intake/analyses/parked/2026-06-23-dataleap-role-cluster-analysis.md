- deploy AI in big corporations;

- find highest-leverage use cases;
- build agents and workflows with customers;

## Why Not Spend Normal Application Slots

The Munich roles are explicit:

- native German;
- based in Munich or ready to move;
- DACH customers;
- on-site workshops and implementation sprints.

The SF role is explicit:

- San Francisco;
- backend-heavy Go / full-stack founding engineer;
- AI coding/product engineer profile;
- very intense early-stage IC path.

The user should not burn normal job-search energy trying to force fit unless the application is deliberately framed as a long-shot.
