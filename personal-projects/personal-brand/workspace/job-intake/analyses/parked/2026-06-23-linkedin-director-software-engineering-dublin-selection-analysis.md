## Pattern Read

This LinkedIn batch is useful as a market sample: AI-native engineering and platform/developer-productivity language is now showing up in director/principal searches, but LinkedIn is pulling it through a Dublin/Ireland geography filter and a lot of current-stack matching.

For the current search strategy, these are not better than the strongest active AI Transformation / Stability & Governance targets already in the intake. Toast is the only role that deserves a second look if the location gate changes.
