## Fit Summary

What fits:

- Forward-deployed shape: customer workflow discovery, scoping, pilots, deployment, product feedback loop.
- Strong match to AI implementation positioning: multi-document processing, AI agents, pipelines, explainability, hallucination control, regulated buyers.
- User's enterprise delivery, document/process automation, AI/MedTech traceability, and production-quality framing are relevant.
- Commercial/presale and customer-facing architecture are relevant.

What blocks:

- `Central London (in person)` is likely a hard blocker.
- They explicitly ask for `2+ years of commercial (re)insurance experience`.
- They ask for a network in insurance/reinsurance, especially the London market.
- The role is first FDE in a tiny startup, so they may need immediate local domain access more than general AI/enterprise strength.

## Decision

Do not put Parsewise into the main active application queue.

Keep as:

- market signal for "AI document processing + regulated workflow + FDE";
- possible long-shot only if applying is nearly zero-cost and the message directly acknowledges the insurance/London-market gap.

## If Applying Anyway

Use profile / AI implementation framing, not CTO-heavy framing.

Short note:

```text

My strongest fit is adjacent rather than identical: I build production AI workflows for regulated/enterprise contexts, from discovery and customer workflow mapping to architecture, integrations, pilots and rollout. I have worked with document-heavy, traceability-sensitive AI scenarios and with enterprise presales/customer implementation, and I tend to focus on hallucination control, explainability, human-in-the-loop and production readiness.

## Questions If They Respond

## Reusable Lesson
