## Risk Mitigation

- Mitigation 1: Frame as data/platform/CRM-integration CTO, not generic CTO.
- Mitigation 2: Be transparent about the domain bridge: no claim of direct traffic arbitration; emphasize fast translation of business metrics into IT metrics.
- Mitigation 3: Use a compact, hands-on cover letter focused on problems: leads, data quality, scoring, integrations, 24/7 reliability.
- Mitigation 4: Ask immediately whether CRM/ERP/data-platform experience is acceptable instead of strict AdTech/CPA.
