EPAM wants a hands-on senior technical delivery leader for Ireland, focused on large complex client programs, primarily in Financial Services. The role combines program execution, delivery quality, client advisory, architecture / technology consulting collaboration, delivery planning, risk management, and pre-sales contribution.

- Secondary track: Digital Transformation / Business Unit Integration
- Seniority: Director / Senior Director
- Role shape: consulting delivery executive with technical-program ownership
- Business context: banking, digital banking, open banking, payments, mobile financial applications

## Match Score

- Leadership: strong; complex multi-stream delivery, stakeholder management, risk and quality ownership are familiar.
- Engineering governance: strong; delivery predictability, architecture, technical execution, and quality expectations fit.
- AI direction: medium-low; EPAM's company positioning is AI-transformation-friendly, but this specific JD is financial-services delivery first.
- Domain: medium-high; fintech / banking / regulated delivery are adjacent, but PSD2, PCI-DSS, open banking and mobile-wallet depth may be screened directly.

## Risks

- Dublin and client-site interaction could make this relocation-heavy.
- Consulting director roles can include substantial account growth, pre-sales, travel, and client-pressure load.
- Salary is hidden; Director / Senior Director consulting economics must beat the current baseline after Irish tax and family relocation costs.
- Specific open-banking, payment, mobile-wallet, PSD2 / PCI-DSS and serverless-mobile requirements may be used as hard filters.
- The role is less aligned with the current AI-first product / solution-architect pivot than remote AI-platform roles.

## Lifestyle / Relocation / Compensation

- Current location fit: weak unless EPAM allows a mostly remote cross-border setup, which the JD does not support.
- Relocation required: likely if pursuing seriously.
- Remote without relocation: not supported by the JD.
- Family / school impact: high; Dublin relocation would reset housing, school, commute, and spouse/family logistics.
- Housing impact: current Novosibirsk housing obligations remain about 86,000 RUB/month; Dublin rent and setup costs would be additional unless the current housing plan changes.
- One-time adaptation costs: high if relocation is required; visa, flights, deposits, documents, schooling transition, and setup costs must be modeled.
- Monthly cost change: likely materially higher than Novosibirsk.
- Market salary benchmark: live web search in this run did not return a usable salary-guide source, so the benchmark is unverified. Treat the role as viable only if the package is materially above the current USD 9k/month contractor baseline after Irish tax and relocation costs.
- Target income: should be comfortably above the current baseline because this is Director / Senior Director, client-facing, regulated-financial-services delivery, and relocation-heavy.
- Quality-of-life verdict: maybe only with strong compensation, visa / relocation support, and manageable client-site expectations.

## Risk Mitigation

- Ask salary band, visa sponsorship, relocation support, office / client-site cadence, and travel expectations before investing.
- Ask whether financial-services domain gaps can be offset by broader fintech / regulated-systems leadership.
- Calibrate messaging toward technical delivery leadership and engineering governance rather than pure CTO scale.
