## Raw JD Summary

Marsh is hiring a Senior IT Director in Marsh Risk Tech to own technology strategy and delivery for a global value-stream portfolio across brokerage value streams: Client, Placement, Policy Servicing, Fiduciary, and Claims. The role reports to the Marsh Risk CIO and emphasizes portfolio strategy, delivery governance, AI-led agile delivery, business-process re-imagination, data / automation / AI, and global team leadership.

## Risk Mitigation

- Ask salary band, visa / relocation support, exact office location options, and whether the three-day hybrid rule is strict.
- Ask how hard the insurance / broker-domain requirement is.
- Ask whether AI-led delivery is a funded transformation program or a generic methodology expectation.
- Position as enterprise transformation + engineering governance, not as a pure insurance-domain specialist.
