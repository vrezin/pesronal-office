## Risks

- Direct holding CIO/CDTO in Russian manufacturing/M&A context is not the canonical positioning; strongest background is CTO/Director Engineering/Transformation.
- Direct 1C ERP architecture, SAP/Axapta replacement and import-substitution ownership is not confirmed. Do not claim deep 1C/SAP/Axapta migration ownership.
- Onsite Moscow 5/2 with 9-hour working day may be a hard constraint; user is in Novosibirsk, so relocation/commute terms must be clarified.
- Salary is not specified; for this scope the compensation can easily be misaligned.
- MBA/business education is only desirable, not hard, but they may prefer classic CIO from industrial holding.

## Risk Mitigation

- Position as technology transformation leader / CIO-level operator who has repeatedly built operating models, governance, support/development processes, architecture and delivery discipline across complex organizations.
- Acknowledge ERP specificity carefully: strong enterprise systems, integrations, transactional systems, financial processes and platform modernization; ready to go deep with 1C/SAP/Axapta teams, but do not pretend to be a career 1C ERP architect.
- Ask early about the real first 6-month priority: audit and governance, ERP target architecture, support centralization, BI/reporting, or AI/automation pilots.
- Ask salary and onsite/relocation before investing in a tailored resume.
