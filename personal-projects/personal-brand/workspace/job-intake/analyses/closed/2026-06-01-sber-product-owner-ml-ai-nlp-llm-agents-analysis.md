## Risks

- Risk 1: JD explicitly asks Product Owner / Product Manager with 3+ years ML/AI-product ownership; user profile may look like CTO/architect rather than PO.
- Risk 2: They may expect hands-on backlog rituals, ICE/RICE, A/B testing, product analytics and internal stakeholder sales as the daily core.
- Risk 3: ML lifecycle language includes data discovery, model monitoring, drift, feature store; direct MLOps product ownership should not be overclaimed.
- Risk 4: 3-6 years experience and onsite/hybrid Moscow can create overqualification and logistics risks.

## Risk Mitigation

- Mitigation 1: Use cover letter to say this is interesting specifically as an AI product scaling role, not as a pure CTO role.
- Mitigation 2: Start with pilots, ROI, business metrics and customer interaction automation, not engineering org size.
- Mitigation 3: Be honest: strong in LLM application/product implementation and enterprise rollout; not positioning as ML researcher or model-training PM.
- Mitigation 4: Ask early about expected PO autonomy, backlog ownership, A/B/product analytics maturity and Moscow presence.
