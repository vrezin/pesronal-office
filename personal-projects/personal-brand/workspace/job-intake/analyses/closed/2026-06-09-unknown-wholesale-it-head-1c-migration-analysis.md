Оптовая торговая компания со складами, филиалами, внутренними учетными платформами, автоматизациями и большим потоком данных ищет руководителя ИТ-блока. - CDEK: IT department leadership for a business-critical logistics platform, stabilization after accumulated technical debt, DBA/query optimization and user support separation.
- Arameem / ToYou: high-load delivery/e-commerce platform with orders, payments, settlements, pricing/operational flows, integrations, reporting and production stability.
- Arameem / ToYou: portfolio/roadmap management, 14 teams/leads, release discipline, quality gates and coordination with business growth.
- Sobit: owner/operator experience in custom development, client requirements, project portfolio, distributed teams, finances and delivery responsibility.
- Fincomtech: product/engineering direction from zero, enterprise presale, architecture, pilots, customer-facing roadmap and practical AI/automation work.

## Risks

- Risk 1: Vacancy explicitly asks for 1С:УТ, 1С:КА, 1С:ERP and migration to 1C; direct hands-on 1C expertise should not be overstated.
- Risk 2: Salary is hidden and the company asks for expectations; broad responsibility may be priced below senior CTO/director level.
- Risk 3: Role may combine strategic migration, support, access management, documentation and day-to-day firefighting into one person.
- Risk 4: Location says Калининград, while conditions say remote and Moscow hours; actual remote policy should be verified.

## Risk Mitigation

- Mitigation 1: Position as Head of IT / business systems leader who can structure requirements, integrations, vendor control, migration governance and operational reliability.
- Mitigation 2: Ask early whether they need a hands-on 1C architect/developer or an IT leader who manages internal experts and contractors.
- Mitigation 3: Give salary expectations as a range after clarifying team size, contractor budget, migration scope and decision authority.
- Mitigation 4: Frame logistics/e-commerce experience as directly relevant to warehouses, orders, stock, pricing, movements, reporting and transport processes.
