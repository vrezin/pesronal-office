## Raw JD Summary

Xapo Bank is creating a new function-level Head of Engineering role under the CTO. The role owns how engineering operates across three product tribes: unified SDLC, engineering standards, delivery predictability, people leadership of Engineering Managers, backend change governance, incident response, tech-debt roadmap, capacity planning, and AI-assisted engineering adoption. The context is a Bitcoin-native regulated bank, so audit-ready change management, incident discipline, observability, quality and security standards are central rather than secondary.
