## Fit Summary

What fits strongly:

- Forward deployed / enterprise implementation role.
- Production go-live ownership, not advisory-only consulting.
- Customer architecture, POC, technical workshops, executive communication.
- Regulated industries: banks, fintechs, insurance, payments.
- Complex enterprise integration with legacy systems.
- Product feedback loop from customer edge cases to roadmap.
- User's CFT/banking background, enterprise delivery, integration architecture, presale, quality/governance and production-readiness experience are relevant.

What is especially attractive:

- Fully remote, with travel rather than office relocation.
- Travel requirement is explicit: 5-10 days/month across Europe.
- Role values switching between engineer, architect, delivery owner and executive communicator.
- The company sells infrastructure/API into regulated enterprise buyers, which is close to the user's strongest non-HH positioning.

Main gaps / risks:

- They may screen hard for current hands-on backend coding in Java/Kotlin/Node.
- OAuth 2.0 / OIDC and Open Banking / payment infrastructure / regulated onboarding may be treated as hard filters.
- European eIDAS/EUDI Wallet knowledge is likely a plus but could become a filter.
- 5-10 travel days/month may be hard depending on legal/visa/travel logistics.
- Compensation is good but not huge for the role intensity.

## Decision

Apply / fast apply after YC profile sanity check.

Use as a strong B+ test of the forward-deployed enterprise implementation lane.

Do not overclaim current hands-on Kotlin/Java depth or eIDAS expertise. Position around:

- enterprise integration;
- regulated banking/payment context;
- API/platform implementation;
- legacy/enterprise architecture;
- production go-live ownership;
- customer-facing delivery and presale.
