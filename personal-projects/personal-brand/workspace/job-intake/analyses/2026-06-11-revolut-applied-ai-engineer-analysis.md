- The posting explicitly wants applied AI, LLM, NLP, and machine-learning delivery.
- The role is product-facing and outcome-driven, which fits your AI implementation framing.
- It includes roadmap and risk ownership, which keeps it above a narrow specialist seat.

- Salary is not shown in the posting, so you need a follow-up before committing.
- LinkedIn shows 100+ applicants clicked apply, so the first message has to be concise and sharply positioned.
