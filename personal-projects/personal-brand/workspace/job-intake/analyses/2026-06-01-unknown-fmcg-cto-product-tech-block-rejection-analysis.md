# Rejection Analysis - Unknown FMCG Tech Product - CTO

- Company: unknown

- Status: rejected after application

## Classification

- Primary track: tailored hybrid of Heavy Enterprise + Stability & Governance + Product Engineering
- Secondary track: CTO / Head of Product Engineering
- Seniority: CTO, but likely hands-on/direct management of compact tech block
- Business context: FMCG analytics / merchandising / ML product for enterprise clients

## Best CV In Current Set

- Best ready CV: `CTO - Head of Engineering - Stability and Governance.pdf` or `CTO - Co-founder CTO - Head of Product Engineering.pdf`.
- Heavy Enterprise may be too large unless they really want 50+ FTE CTO.
- AI Transformation is secondary only if ML/AI is central.

## Match

Strong:

- Technical strategy, architecture, DevOps, QA, integrations, analytics, production stability.
- Team leadership and scaling far above requested 50 FTE.
- High-load product and mobile/backend/integration experience.
- Cost optimization, monitoring, quality gates, release governance.
- Playing manager positioning included in cover letter.

Potential mismatch:

- JD asks for `>25 employees` in responsibilities but `50 FTE` in requirements; likely current team is smaller than user's 200+ org.
- Strong emphasis on specific stack: Go, Python, React, Flutter, YOLO/Triton, Kubernetes, monitoring.
- They may want a more hands-on product CTO in a 25-50 person org, not a large-org executive.
- Cover letter begins with 200+ people, which can trigger overqualification despite later playing-manager mitigation.
- If old/full CV was attached, HH experience/age signal could be too heavy.

## Why Rejection May Have Happened

Most likely:

- wrong attached resume / too enterprise-heavy;
- overqualification signal;
- mismatch with desired hands-on stack depth;
- automated screening on age/years/title;
- company already had a shortlisted candidate.

Less likely:

- cover letter was bad. It was generally good, but led with scale.

## Better CV If Re-Applying / Direct Outreach

Use `CTO - Head of Engineering - Stability and Governance.pdf` if emphasizing:

- production;
- monitoring;
- DevOps;
- Scrum/delivery;
- QA;
- architecture;
- hands-on governance.

Use `CTO - Co-founder CTO - Head of Product Engineering.pdf` if emphasizing:

- portal/mobile/product;
- customer-facing product development;
- smaller team;
- hands-on product engineering.

Ideal tailored headline:

`CTO / Head of Product Engineering`

## Better Cover Letter Variant

```text
Добрый день.

Вакансия близка к моему опыту как CTO / Head of Engineering для продуктовой разработки: портал, мобильное приложение, backend, интеграции, DevOps, QA, аналитика, production stability и работа с бизнес-заказчиками.

Я работаю в формате playing manager: могу держать стратегию и архитектуру, но при этом регулярно заходить в delivery, разбор технических решений, code/design review, работу с тимлидами, метриками, качеством и техническим долгом.

Релевантный опыт:
- управление разработкой high-load платформы с мобильным продуктом, backend, интеграциями, DevOps, QA и аналитикой;
- масштабирование продукта с 3k до 300k DAU и 100k+ заказов в день;
- внедрение CI/CD, unit testing, SonarQube, quality gates и release governance;
- снижение инфраструктурных расходов более чем в 2 раза;
- стабилизация production и модернизация legacy-платформы без остановки бизнеса;
- построение и развитие инженерных руководителей и delivery-процессов.

Мне интересен практический CTO-формат: выстроить предсказуемую разработку, помочь командам быстрее и надежнее поставлять изменения, усилить архитектуру, мониторинг, QA и DevOps-контур, не превращая управление в лишнюю бюрократию.

Буду рад обсудить, какой сейчас размер технического блока, где самые заметные узкие места и какого эффекта от CTO ждут в первые 3-6 месяцев.
```

## Lesson

For compact hands-on CTO roles, do not lead with `200+ people`.

Start with:

- product engineering;
- practical CTO;
- portal/mobile/backend/DevOps/QA;
- production and delivery;
- playing manager;
- then mention scale as proof.

## Decision

- Re-approach: only if direct contact possible or if company is very attractive.
- Future similar roles: use Product Engineering or Stability CV, not Heavy Enterprise.
