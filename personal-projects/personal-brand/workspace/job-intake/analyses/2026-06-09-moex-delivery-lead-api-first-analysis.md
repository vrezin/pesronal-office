## Risks

- Risk 1: Vacancy expects deep integration-platform vocabulary and possibly direct ownership of API Gateway / ESB / MQ / OpenAPI / AsyncAPI / OIDC / SAML tooling; profile has strong architecture and delivery leadership but should not overclaim recent hands-on platform administration.
- Risk 2: Role is labeled Delivery Lead / Руководитель направления разработки, so the recruiter may screen for a narrower integration lead rather than transformation director.
- Risk 3: Salary is absent and the role may require Moscow hybrid presence despite "remote or hybrid" wording.
- Risk 4: Exchange-domain complexity may favor candidates with direct exchange/trading-platform background.

## Risk Mitigation

- Mitigation 1: Frame as enterprise integration-modernization leader: "могу вести программу от стратегии, дорожной карты и бизнес-кейса до изменения SDLC-процессов команд".
- Mitigation 2: Be precise on integration tech: strong understanding of API-first, auth, observability, event/message patterns and platform governance; clarify which parts require hands-on tool administration.
- Mitigation 3: Use banking/core-financial-system experience as the domain bridge, and high-load payment/settlement/accounting flow as the operational bridge.
- Mitigation 4: Ask early about hybrid cadence, budget authority, team composition and compensation range before heavy customization.
