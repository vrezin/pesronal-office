## Raw JD Summary

Пруфикс is an accredited IT company in video communications and AI. It has a 12-year corporate video/events platform and AI products on top, with enterprise clients such as Central Bank of Russia, TELE2, SIBUR, Gazprom, MChS and Vkusno i Tochka.
