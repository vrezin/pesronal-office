Early-stage Delaware-registered social shopping startup looking for a hands-on founding/playing CTO: build MVP almost from scratch, shape architecture, write code, hire and focus the team, integrate AI/ML personalization/analytics, manage cloud infrastructure, and participate in investor/partner conversations.

- Seniority: CTO / founding CTO / playing technical co-founder shape
- Role shape: hands-on architect + senior developer + team lead + product/technical sparring partner
- Business context: early-stage social commerce / peer-to-peer social shopping platform with unclear current traction

## Best CV

- Recommended CV: `CTO - Co-founder CTO - Head of Product Engineering.pdf`
- Use as-is / tailor: use as-is for first response; tailor cover letter strongly toward "playing CTO / MVP / product engineering from zero"
- Tailoring notes: do not lead with 200+ people; foreground Fincomtech/Sobit/operator experience, MVP/product architecture, small-team leadership, customer/investor-facing work, quality/process setup, and AI implementation governance. Mention large-scale Arameem/ToYou experience only as proof that early architecture choices can survive growth.

## Evidence To Use

- Built product/engineering direction from early stage, combining product roadmap, architecture, delivery, presale, and customer feedback loops.
- Scaled high-load product environment from 3k to 300k DAU and 100k+ delivery orders/day, with up to 1M+ financial transactions/day.
- Built engineering processes: quality gates, unit testing, CI/CD, release discipline, code review, incident/stability practices.
- Led teams while staying close to architecture, decomposition, delivery risks, and trade-offs between MVP scope and technical complexity.
- Relevant AI positioning: LLM/AI application architecture, automation, RAG/agents/governance, and pragmatic AI implementation rather than research ML.

## Risks

- Risk 1: "сам активно пишет код", Python, N8N, AWS, AI/ML may mean they want a senior developer with CTO title.
- Risk 2: 6/1 and evening/night shifts are a serious lifestyle and governance red flag, especially for a strategic CTO role.
- Risk 3: Company/product naming inconsistency: Shopinion in most of JD, VivoSocial in application section.
- Risk 4: "innovation patents", Delaware corporation, global potential, huge upside may be mostly fundraising narrative; current traction is not shown.
- Risk 5: Social shopping/e-commerce/social network domain is adjacent but not direct.

## Risk Mitigation

- Mitigation 1: In cover letter, explicitly frame the fit as a playing CTO: architecture, MVP focus, hands-on technical leadership, code review/critical-path coding where needed.
- Mitigation 2: Ask about current product stage, team size, runway, funding, equity terms, and expected code/management ratio before investing much time.
- Mitigation 3: Avoid claiming deep ML training experience; position AI/ML as applied personalization/analytics architecture, vendor/model selection, data flows, experimentation, and production governance.
- Mitigation 4: Clarify schedule expectations early; evening overlap is one thing, permanent 6/1/night mode is another.

- Question 4: Что означает график 6/1 и вечерние/ночные часы: постоянный режим или overlap с США?
- Question 5: Как устроены funding/runway/equity: есть ли инвестиции, на сколько месяцев runway, какой диапазон доли и vesting?
