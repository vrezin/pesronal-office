## Living Standard

- Rent for family-sized housing: Lisbon / Porto pressure is real; 2024 new-contract rents were about 13.16 EUR/m2 in Greater Lisbon and 9.12 EUR/m2 in the Porto metro area, so a family-sized unit can quickly move into four-digit monthly rent before utilities
- School options for two daughters: TBD
- Safety: generally favorable; Portugal sits in the top peaceful group in the 2025 Global Peace Index
- Healthcare: universal tax-funded system with important waiting-time and inequity issues
- Education: TBD

## Relocation Economics

- One-time relocation costs: visa/admin, flights, deposits, temporary housing, school setup, and household reset are all real
- Monthly cost delta vs Novosibirsk: likely materially higher, especially if the family needs private school or private healthcare fallback
- Company support available: unknown
- Relocation without company support: possible only if the salary is comfortably above local market and the family accepts the tradeoff
- Tax / admin notes: Portugal has a backlog-sensitive immigration environment, so paperwork friction is a practical risk

## Hiring Filter Notes
