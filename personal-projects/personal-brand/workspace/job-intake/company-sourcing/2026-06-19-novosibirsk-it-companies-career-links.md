Date: 2026-06-19

Scope: first practical sourcing pass for direct applications and warm/direct outreach. This is not a legal registry of every tiny web studio in Novosibirsk. Priority is companies with meaningful engineering/product work, local Novosibirsk presence, or active Novosibirsk vacancies visible on HH.

## How To Use

- First pass: check `Priority A` and `Priority B` career pages weekly.
- If a career page has no senior role, use LinkedIn/Telegram/direct founder or engineering leader search before going through generic HH filters.
- Treat `HH active signal` as proof that the company is hiring in Novosibirsk now, not as proof that the role is a fit.
- For 1C-heavy/integrator companies, do not spend deep effort unless the role is explicitly delivery/P&L/transformation leadership rather than narrow 1C implementation.
