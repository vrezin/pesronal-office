- Created: 2026-06-19

- Purpose: find AI/Product Engineering/Solution Architecture roles at YC-backed startups

## What It Is

Work at a Startup is YC's startup job board. The public YC jobs page positions it as a way to apply to many startup jobs with a single profile, browse YC companies, and let YC founders contact the candidate.

The live page currently shows startup roles across engineering, product, design, remote jobs, and other functions. Public examples seen on 2026-06-19 include role shapes such as `Founding AI Engineer`, `Founding Product Engineer`, `Head of Engineering`, `Senior AI Engineer`, and ML/agentic-healthcare engineering roles.

## Fit For Current Positioning

Potentially good match for:

- `AI Product Engineer`;
- `AI Solution Architect` when the role is practical/product-facing rather than pure ML research;
- founding or early senior AI/product engineering roles;
- fractional/interim CTO only where the company is already serious enough and not asking for unpaid chaos;
- AI-native SDLC / agent workflows / product automation roles.

Not the main channel for:

- stable enterprise AI transformation roles;
- Russian-market remote roles;
- corporate AI adoption leadership without hands-on builder expectations.

## First-Pass Filters

Access blocker found on 2026-06-19:

- Russian phone country code is absent from the phone-number list during profile/application setup.
- This makes the channel materially less practical unless a legally usable non-Russian phone number is available.
- Do not invest time into full role analysis before confirming that profile creation and application submission are actually possible.

Update later on 2026-06-19:

- User managed to register on Work at a Startup despite the phone-country friction.
- Next step is filling the candidate profile before role review.

Prioritize:

- remote roles that are not restricted to US/GB/EU residence;
- B2B SaaS, healthtech/medtech, developer tools, automation, support/sales/document AI, data/knowledge products;
- title patterns: `AI Product Engineer`, `Founding AI Engineer`, `AI Engineer`, `Forward Deployed AI/ML Engineer`, `Head of Engineering`, `AI Solutions Engineer`, `Product Engineer`;
- companies with visible traction, paying customers, funding/runway, and sane hiring process;
- roles where enterprise engineering, production readiness, SDLC, CI/CD, quality gates, integrations, and agentic workflows are useful rather than baggage.

Reject or park:

- US-only / Canada-only / UK-only roles without visa/relocation path;
- unpaid/co-founder-first roles;
- "send video before a conversation" style asymmetric screening for senior roles;
- deep ML research / model-training roles where the expected profile is a current ML scientist;
- tiny chaos startups asking for CTO responsibility with senior IC salary and no authority;
- roles that require moving the family quickly without clear economics.

## Review Workflow

1. Open Work at a Startup / YC Jobs and search by `AI`, `agent`, `LLM`, `product engineer`, `solution`, `health`, `workflow`, `automation`, `engineering manager`, `head of engineering`.
2. For each interesting role, capture full JD into `job-intake/jd-archive/`.
3. Create a normal analysis in `job-intake/analyses/`.
4. Update `job-intake/INDEX.md` and `COMPANY_NOTES.md` only for concrete reviewed roles.
5. Create per-role active or waiting tasks after a decision.

## Initial Hypothesis

This may be worth a focused review now that registration was completed, but it is still not a clean sourcing channel. Use it only as a curated weekly/biweekly pass for high-signal AI/product/startup roles, with strict location/legal and maturity filters.
