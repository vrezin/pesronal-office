# RF IT Direct Apply Target Map

- Created: 2026-06-17
- Status: Draft sourcing map
- Purpose: build a direct-apply channel for serious Russian IT / digital companies
- Scope: official company career pages first; HH only as fallback evidence

## Operating Rule

Do not treat a company as actionable until its official career page and at least one relevant vacancy or open application path is verified.

Every real vacancy still goes through the normal job-intake flow:

1. save full JD to `job-intake/jd-archive/`;
2. save analysis to `job-intake/analyses/`;
3. update `INDEX.md`;
4. create or update a per-vacancy task.

## Target Role Filters

Prioritize roles that fit the current CV portfolio:

- AI Transformation Lead / AI Automation Architect;
- Head / Lead of AI implementation;
- AI platform / GenAI / agents / RAG leadership;
- Director / Head of Engineering for platform or product engineering;
- CTO / VP Engineering / Head of Development where the scope is not pure maintenance;
- Digital / Technology Transformation Director;
- Stability, SDLC, engineering governance, delivery excellence when the company is large enough.

Avoid by default:

- junior/middle hands-on IC roles;
- pure 1C/internal automation unless leadership scope is strong;
- narrow project manager roles without technical ownership;
- local-only office roles with unclear compensation and no strategic upside.

## Direct Apply Workflow

For each company:

1. Find official career page.
2. Record career URL and application mechanics: native form, email, HH redirect, referral, open application.
3. Search role keywords: `AI`, `GenAI`, `LLM`, `RAG`, `агенты`, `платформа`, `руководитель разработки`, `head of engineering`, `трансформация`, `SDLC`, `delivery`.
4. If role is real and relevant, archive the JD and analyze normally.
5. If no role exists but company is strategically good, add it to a weekly revisit list instead of forcing an application.
