How I Run an Implementation

The goal is not to add AI for its own sake. The goal is to create a workflow people can actually use after the demo is over.

Diagnosis

We examine the process, roles, data, constraints, current time losses, and success criteria.

Scenario Selection

We separate AI scenarios from ordinary automation and choose the first testable use case.

Pilot

We build a prototype on real examples and check quality, errors, and operational risks.

Deployment

Integrations, instructions, user training, and human approval for sensitive actions.

Scaling

New scenarios, knowledge base updates, metrics, support, and controlled growth.
