# Surface Strategy For Workflow-First OS

- Created: 2026-06-10
- Status: someday
- Area: personal-projects / personal-brand / AI-OS packaging

## Purpose

Decide whether Personal Office needs its own user-facing surface, or should mainly reuse existing Google/Yandex-style surfaces for input and notifications.

## Working Hypothesis

- Existing ecosystems are strong enough for input, notifications, and daily habit surfaces.
- Personal Office should own the workflow brain, state, routing, and durable truth.
- A custom surface only makes sense where it improves action, status, or review more than existing products do.

## Decision Questions

- What user behavior is impossible or too awkward in Google/Yandex surfaces?
- Which part of the workflow needs a dedicated cockpit?
- What can stay invisible and still work well?
- What is the smallest custom surface worth building?
