## Goal

Decide whether to respond to an unknown-company `Инженер по AI-автоматизации` vacancy despite the explicit `нахождение вне РФ и РБ` requirement.

## First-Pass Verdict

Content fit is strong, practical eligibility is mixed.

The work maps well to the current target direction:

- multi-step AI scenarios with memory, tools and quality control;
- LLM + backend + DB + queues + internal services;
- AI systems moving from prototype to scalable production;
- monitoring and business logic;
- daily AI-agent tools such as Claude Code, Codex and Cursor.

The explicit blocker is location: the vacancy asks for candidates outside Russia and Belarus.

Important nuance: the user has an individual entrepreneur legal entity in Kyrgyzstan for B2B contracting. This may solve or reduce the contract/legal setup issue, but it does not automatically solve a hard physical-location requirement.

## Recommendation

Default: quick clarification is now reasonable, but do not invest deeply unless they confirm that KG B2B legal setup is acceptable despite current physical location.

Proceed only if one of these is true:

- the contact says the `outside RF/RB` filter is about legal/payment setup rather than physical presence;
- Kyrgyzstan ИП is acceptable for B2B contracting;
- the role is unusually well-paid B2B and worth a short eligibility clarification.
