## Objective

Prepare the concrete position and safe disclosure boundary for a Reksoft / Positive Technologies expert validation of Fincom potential.

## Current Read

Reksoft is likely too broad and strong to approach as a generic competitor. Clarified frame: they may be pulled in as external experts to evaluate whether Fincom has real potential. Treat this as investor-side due diligence with possible evaluator conflict of interest, not as a friendly partner brainstorm.

Fincom AI-board verdict on 2026-06-23: `accepted_with_conditions`. Proceed with product demo only, not technical audit / partnership workshop / implementation deep dive. Show coherent product scenario, buyer pain, visible outputs, high-level integration contours, readiness labels, and pilot-style next step. Block internals, detailed architecture, code, data mappings, prompts/RAG/agent internals, customer-sensitive artifacts, financial model, valuation, unsupported full-replacement/security/autonomous-AI claims.
