# Build RF IT Company Direct Apply List

- Created: 2026-06-17

## Objective

Create a practical list of serious Russian IT / digital companies, verify their official vacancy sections, and route suitable roles through the existing job-intake process for direct applications.

## Why

This creates an outbound search channel independent of HH recommendations and recruiter inboxes. It should improve coverage for senior AI, platform, engineering leadership, and transformation roles that may be easier to find on company career pages than in generic feeds.

## Next Actions

1. Verify official career pages for the first 12 companies in the sourcing map.
2. Record career URL, application method, and whether the site has relevant senior AI / engineering / transformation roles.
3. For every relevant vacancy, save the full JD into `job-intake/jd-archive/` and create the normal analysis/task/index entries.
4. Park companies with no current fit into a revisit list instead of creating noisy vacancy tasks.

## Done When

- At least 20 target companies are listed.
- At least 12 official career pages are verified.
- Relevant direct-apply vacancies are either routed into job-intake or explicitly marked as no current fit.
- The direct-apply workflow has a repeatable weekly revisit rule.
