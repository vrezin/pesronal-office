## Problem

We have a strong intake/archive/processed pipeline, but we do not yet have a living action queue that answers:

- what needs attention now;
- what is waiting for someone else;
- what is blocked;
- what is stale;
- what can be ignored.

The system can store context correctly and still feel dumb if it does not continuously surface the next actions.

## Goal

Define the smallest useful action queue for Personal Office and decide where it should live.

## Deliverables

- a short definition of the action queue;
- the top-level buckets it must contain;
- the minimum fields each item needs;
- the first file or surface that should show it.

## Next Step

Review the current inbox/processed gap analysis and turn the gap into a concrete operating queue.
