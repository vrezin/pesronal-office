## Recommended Positioning

- CTO for complex production systems, high-load platforms, transaction reliability, engineering governance, AI integration and building from zero.
- Adjacent but honest bridge to trading/HFT: do not claim direct HFT production ownership.
- Strongest evidence: high-load transactional platform, 1M+ financial transactions/day contour, infrastructure optimization, quality/release governance, GenAI production integration, fintech/banking foundation.

## Questions To Ask

- Насколько прямой опыт HFT/trading infrastructure является обязательным фильтром?
- Какие latency targets, markets and asset classes are in scope?
- Что именно входит в software layer for new hardware: runtime, network stack, GPU/FPGA/custom accelerators, inference hardware?
- Кто в текущей команде уже закрывает HFT/quant/trading domain expertise?
- Какой ожидаемый результат CTO за первые 3-6 месяцев?
- Какой compensation range, option structure, vesting/cliff, relocation and legal employment setup?
