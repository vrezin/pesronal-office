# Build Relocation Region Shortlist

- Area: personal-projects / personal-brand / job-search economics
- Related workspace: `personal-projects/personal-brand/workspace/job-intake/relocation/`
- Status: active

## Objective

Turn the family relocation criteria into a region-first shortlist, then deepen the shortlist into city-level country cards and school/healthcare checks.

## Current Criteria

- climate not hot, ideally 18-28 C;
- accessible, quality healthcare;
- good schools for two daughters;
- safety that allows real child freedom;
- affordability that does not break family economics.
- family language/adaptation speed, with English-friendly daily life preferred for faster settling.
- practical visa accessibility for a Russian family, especially employer-sponsored work routes and family follow-on.
- income/tax override: weaker climates or harder languages can still be acceptable if the package and tax base make the move rational.

## First-Pass Shortlist

- North Spain
- Netherlands
- Denmark
- Ireland
- Northern Portugal

## Red-Flag Framing

- do not treat Cyprus as a universal negative; treat it as a boundary case where climate/service comfort is close to the limit;
- do not treat “not in shortlist” as “bad country”; it only means the current family fit is weaker;
- any country can move up if salary, tax, housing, school access, and relocation support offset the weaker axis.
- always distinguish country average from city pocket; a country can be red-flagged on macro level but still have livable neighborhoods or cities.
