## Goal

Check Work at a Startup daily for new high-signal roles while the job search is in cashflow-first mode.

## What To Look For

Priority lanes:

- AI Product Engineer / AI Solution Architect / Responsible AI / HealthTech;
- CTO / Head of Engineering / Director roles with strong product or platform scope;
- super-app, delivery, e-commerce, fintech, payroll, payments, marketplaces;
- remote or legally feasible roles with sponsorship / relocation package;
- roles likely to move fast enough to help cashflow.

## Daily Action

Open Work at a Startup and inspect new roles.

For each strong role:

1. save JD to `personal-projects/personal-brand/workspace/job-intake/jd-archive/`;
2. create analysis in `personal-projects/personal-brand/workspace/job-intake/analyses/`;
3. update `INDEX.md` and `COMPANY_NOTES.md`;
4. create active or waiting task.

## Stop Rule

Do not doom-scroll. Stop after either:

- 3 strong candidates;
- 20 minutes;
- no materially relevant new roles.
