## Interpretation

This looks less like a simple full IT-team replacement and more like a broad transformation / build-out:

- Moscow-facing architecture / leadership layer: CTO and 1C enterprise architect;
- Sakhalin onsite digital delivery pod: project manager plus Python developer working close to business customers;
- AI / LLM / agentic systems are named in both AI Product Lead and CTO contexts;
- HR transformation is also being hired for, suggesting company-wide organizational change.

## Risk Read

Yellow / amber, not automatic red.

Positive:

- real industrial company with meaningful operational processes;
- strong need for applied AI, internal products, agents and decision-support systems;
- likely high business impact if the mandate is real.

Concerning:

- many postings use language like `build from scratch`, `transform`, `next level`, `near customer`, `high dynamics`;
- some key roles require relocation to Sakhalin for at least one year;
- salary is hidden;
- unclear whether Moscow/remote is real for senior technology roles;
- possible transformation chaos and expectation that a few senior people will compensate for weak existing systems.

## Practical Implication

If continuing with VGK, screen hard before deeper commitment:

- Are AI Product Lead and CTO separate roles or alternatives?
- Who owns the onsite Sakhalin digital pod?
- Is relocation ever expected for the AI/CTO role?
- What is the approved team/budget?
- Is this a new build-out, replacement of existing leadership, or rescue of troubled initiatives?
- What are the first 3-6 month success metrics?
