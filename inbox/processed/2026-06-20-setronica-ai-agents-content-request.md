# Setronica AI Agents Content Request

Date: 2026-06-20

Source: Slack/DM excerpt from Alexandra Kashun, content manager at Setronica, referred by Olya Yakushkina / brand executive.

## Request

Alexandra asked whether Vladimir is interested in participating in content for Setronica corporate blog and LinkedIn:

- what he does with the company;
- AI agents implementation;
- successes and failures;
- paradigm shift in development;
- practical material in the spirit of `ai-agents`.

Alexandra offered two formats:

- Vladimir writes the text, Alexandra edits/translates/structures;
- Alexandra interviews Vladimir / takes answers and drafts the article, Vladimir verifies.

User replied:

- he is not an employee and does not know the full internal Setronica picture;
- he knows one specific team/project plus Yulia and several key people;
- he is open, especially if signed by his own name;
- his main focus is development management, now transferring that experience to AI agents;
- proposed starting with written questions, then discussing by call.

Alexandra agreed to send questions on Monday.

## User Content Direction

User is considering turning the current reflection into a Setronica-relevant article/cycle around:

- AI agents accelerate engineering work;
- the bottleneck shifts from code to decision ownership;
- coordination debt;
- executable organization;
- AI does not replace managerial will / decision ownership.

The latest stronger variant shifts toward:

> AI не создал проблему управления. Он просто сорвал занавеску.

## Assessment

This is a good personal-brand and Setronica thought-leadership opportunity, but it should not expose specific internal project details without review.

Recommended frame:

- Signed by Vladimir personally.
- Use Setronica as platform / publication channel, not as claim that Vladimir represents the whole company.
- Focus on general lessons from AI-assisted delivery and organizational design.
- Avoid naming specific projects, clients, internal blockers, or people.
