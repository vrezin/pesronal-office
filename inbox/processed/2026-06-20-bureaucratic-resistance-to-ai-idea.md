# Bureaucratic Resistance To AI Idea

Date: 2026-06-20

User observed that for a bureaucrat, adopting AI may be almost contraindicated because an agent can replace much of bureaucratic routine.

Captured as a sensitive theme:

- AI threatens bureaucratic rent, not healthy governance;
- the organization may benefit from AI, but bureaucratic layers may lose status/control when routine coordination becomes automated;
- resistance may present as risk management while protecting manual, opaque, slow process.
