# Meeting Theater Idea

Date: 2026-06-20

User provided two anecdotes:

- a familiar manager from a VTB-related interview context bragged that his calendar was packed all day, sometimes with two or three meetings simultaneously, and that he connected to all remotely but did not listen;
- in a Sber-related meeting, eight people joined from one side, seven of whom stayed silent the whole time.

Captured as the `meeting theater` theme:

- calendar load as status;
- attendance without attention;
- quorum as crowd, not decision capacity;
- meetings as risk-distribution ritual;
- AI meeting tools can summarize discussion but cannot create decision ownership.
