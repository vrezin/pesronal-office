## Change

The one-pager was restructured because the previous version started to contradict its own product logic:

- it opened with architecture and broad product scope;
- the strongest buyer-facing idea appeared too late;
- the first sellable wedge and pilot were buried near the bottom;
- technical capabilities risked competing with the actual promise.

## New Structure

The document now leads with:

1. one-sentence product;
2. target buyer;
3. main pain;
4. first sellable product;
5. first pilot;
6. user-facing value;
7. how it works for the user;
8. SaaS / ecosystem positioning;
9. technical architecture as supporting proof.

## Intent

The new structure should make the document easier to read as a product hypothesis:

> Private AI Office sells cognitive-load reduction and context continuity first; architecture, agents, and small software generation explain how the promise is delivered.
