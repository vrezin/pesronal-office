## Situation

Fincom sees АйТуБи as a potentially strategic counterparty: a large company with money and many incoming customer orders that they may not be able to cover alone.

The immediate DCIM/ITAM opportunity is one concrete entry point, but the strategic issue is broader: how to make Fincom a preferred/key execution partner for АйТуБи's future order flow without giving away economics, governance, or equity on bad terms.

## Proposed / Discussed Mechanisms

1. Profit share per contract sourced by АйТуБи.
2. Treating part of profit from АйТуБи-sourced contracts as АйТуБи's investment into Fincom, later converting into company equity.
3. A clearer option-like structure: after a defined period, АйТуБи may acquire X% of the company for amount X, paid upfront or over time.

## Initial Concerns

- АйТуБи will already resell Fincom's work and keep commercial margin, so an extra profit share may double-pay the channel.
- "Profit share becomes investment becomes equity" can become legally, accounting-wise, and governance-wise messy.
- Profit is easy to dispute unless definitions, cost allocation, taxes, collection, receivables, and project accounting are tightly specified.
- Equity transfer based on future sourced projects could create incentives to push bad-margin work or distort Fincom's cap table.
- A strategic option may be useful, but only if it buys real commitment: pipeline access, exclusivity/preferred supplier status, minimum order volume, co-selling, prepayment, investment, or delivery leverage.

## Framing Correction - 2026-06-14

User clarified that, in theory, an option-like mechanism is needed by Fincom, not by АйТуБи.

This reframes the question:

- The purpose is not to give АйТуБи an extra benefit because they need it.
- The purpose is to solve Fincom's problem: how to make АйТуБи treat Fincom as a strategically important/default execution partner for future order flow.
- Any mechanism must therefore be evaluated as a Fincom-side channel-retention / alignment tool, not as a concession.
- If АйТуБи already earns reseller/prime margin, extra economics must buy something incremental and measurable for Fincom: repeatable pipeline, access, priority, prepayment, committed volume, or strategic cooperation.
- If the mechanism does not change АйТуБи behavior in Fincom's favor, it is unnecessary margin leakage or cap-table risk.

## Board Question

Should Fincom offer АйТуБи any option-like or investment-linked mechanism to become a strategic/key executor for their order flow?

If yes, what structure is rational and safe:

- preferred supplier / framework agreement;
- volume-based discount or rebate;
- referral / channel margin;
- revenue share;
- profit share;
- warrant/option for equity;
- convertible investment;
- strategic cooperation agreement without equity;
- staged pilot leading to investment rights;
- no special economics until a pipeline threshold is proven.
