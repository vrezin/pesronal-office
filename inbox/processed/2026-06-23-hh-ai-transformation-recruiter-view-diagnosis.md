Resume: `AI Transformation Lead / AI Automation Architect`
Status: processed

## Diagnosis

The HH recruiter view breaks the intended positioning.

The strongest adapted content is present, but HH places it too late:

- the `Обо мне` section appears after all experience, skills and languages;
- the first screen is dominated by contact block, relocation constraint, title, generic specializations and the first job;
- the first visible role is `CTO / сооснователь`, not AI implementation / AI solution architecture;
- `не готов к переезду` appears before the value proposition;
- the Aram Meem 200+ engineering-org proof appears before the recruiter has understood why the candidate fits an AI role.

Practical consequence: all previous adaptation work is not lost, but much of it is invisible in the first recruiter scan.

## What Recruiter Sees First

Before seeing the actual AI positioning, the recruiter sees:

- name and contacts;
- Novosibirsk / no relocation;
- title: `AI Transformation Lead / AI Automation Architect`;
- specializations: CIO / development group lead / CTO;
- experience length;
- current company: `ООО Финкомтех`;
- role: `CTO / сооснователь`;
- a few project bullets.

This can easily be read as:

> senior CTO / founder trying to reposition into AI,

instead of:

> practical AI solution / implementation leader who can take LLM/RAG/agent use cases into production.

## First-Screen Fix

HH first screen must carry the full message without relying on `Обо мне`.

The current title is too abstract for HH:

`AI Transformation Lead / AI Automation Architect`

Better title options:

1. `AI Solution Architect / Head of AI Implementation`
2. `AI Solution Architect / AI Implementation Lead`
3. `Head of AI Implementation / AI Solution Architect`

Recommended for HH:

`AI Solution Architect / Head of AI Implementation`

Reason: this is more concrete for recruiters and maps to visible market terms: solution architecture, implementation, AI delivery.

## Recommended HH Profile Shape

Do not rely on `Обо мне` as the main sales block.

5. `Обо мне` remains useful for people who scroll, but not required for understanding the fit.
