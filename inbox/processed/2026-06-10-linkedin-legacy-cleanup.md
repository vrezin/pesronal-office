# LinkedIn Legacy Cleanup

- Date: 2026-06-10
- Scope: LinkedIn mail before 2026-01-01

## What Was Cleaned

- Archived old LinkedIn messages that were still hanging around in Inbox or All Mail.
- Removed `UNREAD` and `IMPORTANT` from the old LinkedIn thread set so they no longer surface as active noise.

## Result

- No LinkedIn messages before 2026 remain in Inbox.
- No LinkedIn messages before 2026 remain marked unread or important.
- The active LinkedIn intake surface is now the 2026 scan note and the job-intake analyses for the fresh vacancies only.
